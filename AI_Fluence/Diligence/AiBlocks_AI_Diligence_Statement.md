# AiBlocks — AI Diligence Statement

In the development of the **AiBlocks** platform — a multi-ecosystem marketplace for reusable AI components — I engaged in extensive collaboration with Claude (Opus 4.7) from Anthropic.

The base content for this project came from:

- My own product vision, market hypothesis, and strategic direction for AiBlocks as a cross-ecosystem marketplace for AI building blocks (use cases, agents, MCP servers, CLAUDE.md configs, hooks, and related components).
- My research and observations about the current AI tooling landscape across Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, and Deepseek ecosystems, and the gap in reusable component distribution.
- Domain knowledge of marketplace dynamics, developer ecosystems, and platform business models (e.g., npm, GitHub Marketplace, app stores).
- The **AI Fluency 4D Framework** (Delegation, Description, Discernment, Diligence) developed by Rick Dakan (Ringling College of Art and Design) and Joseph Feller (University College Cork) in collaboration with Anthropic, which informed how AI was leveraged across this project.

Throughout this process, Claude assisted me with structural development, idea stress-testing, trade-off analysis, technology evaluation, content drafting, critiquing, editing, and rewriting across the following planning artifacts:

- `AiBlocks_Vision.md` — product vision and core decisions
- `AiBlocks_Page_Mapping.md` — complete page and feature map
- `AiBlocks_Tech_Stack.md` — technology and infrastructure decisions
- `AiBlocks_MVP_Scoping.md` — v1/v2/v3 scope definition
- `AiBlocks_Delegation_Plan.md` — application of the 4D Framework to the build

I wrote, designed, edited, and provided continual vision, expertise, critical judgment, and domain knowledge, and made all final decisions about both content and approach. This includes — but is not limited to — the platform name (AiBlocks, chosen with explicit trademark-risk consideration over alternatives such as AiLego), the 0% launch platform fee, the hard paywall model for paid components, the open-submission community-quality approach, the deferral of versioning and admin tooling to later versions, and the final tech stack selections (Next.js, Tailwind, Supabase, Clerk, Stripe Connect, Vercel).

All AI-generated and co-created content underwent thorough validation, editing, and curation by me. The final materials accurately reflect my understanding, strategic intent, and product direction for AiBlocks. While AI assistance was instrumental in accelerating the production of these planning documents, I maintain responsibility for the content, direction, and any decisions executed under the AiBlocks name.

This disclosure is made in the spirit of transparency advocated by the AI Fluency Framework and to acknowledge the evolving role of AI in product development, planning, and other creative and intellectual work.
